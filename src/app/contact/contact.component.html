Frequently Asked Questions


Purpose of your website:

Q1: “Please briefly summarize your goals for the site?”

What problem do you want to solve that'll make your business management easier?


Q2: “Describe your target audience/market?”

What kind of crowd are you after?


Q3: “What type of content will your site have?”

New updates? Review articles? Product/service promotional content? Personal/individual blogging?


Q4: “What action(s) do you want your visitors to take?”

Purchase product/service? Click advertising? Request quote? Subscribe to newsletter?


Q5: “In two or three words, describe the feel you want?”

Slick and professional? Bright and fun?


Q6: “List two or three colors you want?”

Ex: (Navy blue and white), (middle blue and orange), (Green, yellow and black)


Q7. “What are some example sites that you like?”

There are tons of websites online and a lot of them have amazing creativity and complexity. Feel free to search online for some awsome website ideas if you'd like. If not, that's totally fine, we can build custom websites from our planning.
